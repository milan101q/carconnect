How this works: Each link opens Facebook Marketplace's vehicle search already filtered to your query, price, year, mileage and sort for that area. No scraping, no login automation, nothing that risks your account.
How to get the exact location for any town (do this once per area)
  1. Open facebook.com/marketplace and click the location button (top-left).
  2. Type your town (e.g. "Arlington, Virginia" — pick the right state from the dropdown), set a radius, hit Apply.
  3. Now do any search. Look at the address bar — the URL is facebook.com/marketplace/SOMETHING/search?...
  4. Copy that whole URL and paste it into "Grab From URL" above. Done — that location is now guaranteed correct.

Tip: hit Verify on each saved area — it opens that location's Marketplace so you can confirm it's the right city before generating links.

Why not a fully automatic scraper?

Facebook has no public Marketplace API; automated listing scraping violates their Terms, breaks on every layout change, trips CAPTCHAs and login walls, and can get your account restricted. This builder gives the same practical result — a tidy list of links to filtered results — without the risk.
